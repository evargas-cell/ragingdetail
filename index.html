Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before you book. Don't see your question? Send us a message — we respond same day.

What exactly is mobile detailing?

We bring a full car-cleaning and restoration service to you — whether you're at home, work, or anywhere in between. It's more than a quick car wash: we deep-clean, decontaminate, and protect the interior and exterior of your vehicle on-site.

What services do you offer?

We do everything from express washes and interior deep cleans to paint correction, ceramic coatings, headlight restoration, odor removal, and engine bay cleaning. If you don't see something listed, ask — we probably do it.

How long will it take?

It depends on the service, size of the vehicle, and the vehicle's condition. An express detail can take 30–60 minutes; a full interior/exterior detail is usually 2–4 hours; paint correction or ceramic coating can take a full day. We'll give a realistic time estimate when you book.

How much does it cost?

Prices vary by service, vehicle size, and condition. We list starting prices on our site, but for an accurate quote it's best to send photos or book a quick walk-around estimate. No surprise fees — any extra work is approved by you first.

Do I need to provide water or power?

Although appreciated, no — convenience means we come prepared. We bring everything needed to complete the job.

Where do you service?

We operate around the Metro Atlanta area. If you're unsure whether we service your area, enter your address on the booking page or send us a message — if it's close, we'll try to accommodate you.

How do I book an appointment?

Use the online booking form, call/text us at (678) 756-4540, or email info@ragingdetail.com. Pick a time, choose your service, and we'll confirm. For detailed jobs, we might ask for photos first.

What's your cancellation or rescheduling policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations (under 24 hours) may incur a small fee to cover the time our techs set aside. We'll always try to be flexible if something comes up.

What if it rains?

Light rain usually won't stop us — but if you prefer to reschedule, we completely understand. We'll contact you ASAP and find the next available slot.

Do you guarantee your work?

Yes. We stand behind our services. If you're not satisfied, let us know within 48 hours and we'll make it right — either by reworking the area or offering a refund/discount depending on the situation.

How should I prepare my vehicle?

Remove personal items and valuables before we arrive. If you can't, that's okay — we'll work around them, but we can't be responsible for loss of items left in the car.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, major credit/debit cards, and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Venmo, CashApp, etc.). Payment is due at completion unless other arrangements are made during booking.

Will you clean pet hair and stains?

Yes — we offer pet-hair removal and specialized stain treatments. Pet hair can be stubborn and may require extra time or multiple sessions, but we'll give an honest assessment and price up front.

Can you remove scratches or swirl marks?

We can reduce or remove many light-to-moderate swirls and scratches with paint correction. Deep scratches that have penetrated the clear coat may need bodywork. We'll inspect and recommend the best option.

How long does a ceramic coating last?

Properly applied professional ceramic coatings typically last 1–5 years, depending on the product and maintenance. We'll explain care instructions and offer maintenance packages to extend protection.

Do you offer fleet or recurring service discounts?

Yes — we offer discounted rates for fleets and recurring detailing plans. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do I have to pick from a package?

No — if your vehicle needs more or less than what's listed in one of our packages, we can make accommodations. The packages are intended to make the process easier for both you and us, not to limit you.

How do I get in touch after service if I have an issue?

Call or text (678) 756-4540, or email info@ragingdetail.com. We respond quickly and will address any concerns right away.
