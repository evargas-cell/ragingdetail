# Marketing Audit: Raging Detail
**URL:** https://www.ragingdetail.com  
**Date:** April 2026  
**Type:** Local Business — Mobile Car Detailing  
**Score: 33/100 (Grade: F)**

---

## Executive Summary

Raging Detail scored 33/100 — primarily due to an almost total absence of conversion architecture, SEO fundamentals, and trust signals on the original Squarespace site. The business has real strengths: clear 4-tier pricing, a compelling mobile-first differentiator, a respectable add-ons menu, and a genuine passion-driven origin story. However, none of these strengths were communicated effectively. The biggest single gap is conversion: there was no hero CTA, no above-fold booking mechanism, and no value proposition. The rebuild addresses all 21 identified issues, completely rebuilding the site's messaging, structure, SEO foundations, and design.

## Remaining Recommendations (for client to implement)

1. **Replace gallery placeholders** with real before/after photos from Instagram
2. **Add real Google review count** to trust bar (e.g. "127 Google Reviews")
3. **Add owner photo** to About section (`andres-photo.jpg`)
4. **Replace testimonial placeholders** with real customer reviews
5. **Connect contact form** to a backend (Formspree, EmailJS, or Square Forms)
6. **Add Instagram embeds** — paste specific post embed codes into the IG feed grid
7. **Set up Google Business Profile** if not already done — will generate reviews
8. **Consider a blog** — 4–6 posts on "How often should I detail my car in Atlanta?" etc.
